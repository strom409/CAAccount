You can bind the DevExpress Blazor TreeList to flat data structures. To bind flat data structures, you must specify the following properties:

* [Data](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxTreeList.Data) — A data source.
* [KeyFieldName](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxTreeList.KeyFieldName) — A field that stores unique identifiers for nodes.
* [ParentKeyFieldName](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxTreeList.ParentKeyFieldName) — A field that contains references to parent nodes.
