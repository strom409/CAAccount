The DevExpress Blazor [Polar Chart](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxPolarChart-1) component can use multiple series types to display data. The following series types are available:

* [Line](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxPolarChartLineSeries-3)
* [Area](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxPolarChartAreaSeries-3)
* [Bar](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxPolarChartBarSeries-3)
* [StackedBar](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxPolarChartStackedBarSeries-3)
* [Scatter](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxPolarChartScatterSeries-3)

Use the drop-down menu to change series type.
