
The DevExpress Blazor [Progress Bar](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxProgressBar) allows you to communicate status information associated with ongoing processes.

Use the [Value](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxProgressBar.Value) property to specify the current progress bar value. [MinValue](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxProgressBar.MinValue) and [MaxValue](https://docs.devexpress.com/Blazor/DevExpress.Blazor.DxProgressBar.MaxValue) properties limit the range of accepted values.
